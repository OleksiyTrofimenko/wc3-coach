# Ontology (reference)

Short human-readable cross-reference; the authoritative numbers live in Postgres
(owned by data-keeper). Keep entries patch-tagged.

## Races
- HU (Human), ORC, NE (Night Elf), UD (Undead)

## Per entity, track
- Units: hp, mana, armor type+value, attack type, dps, gold, lumber, food, build time, tech req
- Buildings: hp, armor, gold, lumber, build time, provides
- Heroes: primary attr, abilities (per level), stat growth
- Creep camps: position, difficulty, units, drops (per map)
