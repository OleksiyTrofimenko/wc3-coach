# Matchup: Orc vs Night Elf (OvNE) — patch 2.0

**Provenance:** High-ladder community knowledge, W3Champions analyses.
`verified: community`. Added by Strategist in T3.1.

## Standard Orc build order (OvNE) — Blademaster fast-harass opener

1. 5 peons start mining
2. ~0:15 — peon builds Altar of Storms
3. ~0:30 — peon builds Barracks
4. ~1:00 — Barracks complete → queue Blademaster
5. ~1:00 — peon builds Orc Burrow (food supply)
6. ~1:30 — Blademaster exits → creep small camp or harass wisps
7. ~2:00 — Stronghold (T2) upgrade begins (requires Barracks + Great Hall)
8. ~2:10 — optional 2nd Burrow for food headroom
9. ~3:30 — Stronghold complete → Raiders/Shaman/T2 upgrades available
10. ~5:30 — Expansion (map-dependent)

## Standard Night Elf build order (NEvO) — Demon Hunter / Firelord opener

1. 5 wisps start entangle (mine)
2. ~0:15 — wisp builds Altar of Elders
3. ~0:30 — wisp builds Ancient of War
4. ~1:00 — Altar → hero (Demon Hunter or Tavern Firelord)
5. ~1:00 — 2 Moon Wells for food
6. ~1:45 — hero out → small creep camp
7. ~2:30 — Tree of Ages (T2) begins
8. ~4:30 — Ancient of Lore for Dryads (vs Orc mass-casters)
9. ~6:00 — Expansion

## Win conditions — Orc

- Blade harass wins time and XP; do NOT over-commit
- Power spike: Stronghold + Shaman Bloodlust + 6–8 Raiders at ~5:30–6:00
- Deny expansion: Kodo Drum + Raider ensnare kills Moonwells/expansions

## Win conditions — Night Elf

- Micro-intensive: kite blade with archers, deny XP
- Power spike: Dryads (dispel + slow poison) counter Bloodlust and mass-caster
- Faerie Fire + Archer harassment drains Orc resources

## Common Orc mistakes

- Delaying Stronghold: loses the Shaman/Raider spike window
- Over-creeping blade before 5:00: gives NE free expansion
- Floating gold: not buying 2nd hero or upgrades after Stronghold
- No burrows: supply block stalls production right at the power spike

## Common Night Elf mistakes

- Not harassing blade while it creeps: gives free XP
- Skipping Moon Wells: food block prevents army build-up
- Tech-rushing Ancient of Lore before Moon Wells: food block
- Delayed Dryads vs Bloodlust Orc: loses the T2 fight
