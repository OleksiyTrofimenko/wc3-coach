## Standard Orc build order (OvH) — Blademaster opener

```
0:00   5 peons start mining
0:15   peon → build Altar of Storms
0:30   peon → build Barracks
~0:45  resume 6th peon
1:00   Barracks complete → Blademaster
1:00   peon → build Orc Burrow (food)
1:30   Blademaster out → harass Human base or creep small camp
2:00–2:15  Stronghold upgrade begins (requires Barracks + Great Hall)
2:30   2nd Orc Burrow if at 20 food
3:15   Stronghold complete → Shaman / Raider / T2 upgrades
3:30–4:00  2nd hero (Far Seer or Shadow Hunter)
4:00–4:30  Voodoo Lounge for item access
6:00   Expansion (map-dependent; FE Orc goes earlier ~4:00 if BM pressure worked)
```

Orc vs Human typically follows a **BM-harass-into-T2** pattern. The BM aims to
punish Human workers and delay the Human FE (fast expand), which Human relies on
heavily. If BM harassment forces a delayed FE or kills 2+ peasants, Orc has achieved
a resource lead.

Variant — **Fast Expo Orc (FE):** Stronghold delayed until after expo secured (~3:00).
Risky if Human MK/AM counters early; only viable on safe-expo maps.

## Standard Human build order (HvO) — Archmage FE opener

```
0:00   5 peasants start mining; 1 peasant → Altar of Kings
0:10   militia out (defending vs early BM harass)
0:30   2nd peasant → Barracks
0:45   3rd peasant → Blacksmith (required for Keep upgrade)
1:10   Altar complete → Archmage
1:30   Archmage out → creep with militia escort, or defend BM harass
2:00   3rd Farm for food
2:30   Barracks complete → Mountain King, or hold for eco
2:45–3:00  Town Hall → Keep upgrade begins (requires Barracks + Blacksmith done)
         [Keep: 225 gold, 100 lumber, 75 s build; standard reference ~3:00–3:30]
4:00–4:30  Expansion (Archmage Brilliance Aura + early expo is the HvO power pattern)
5:30–6:00  Sorceresses / Spellbreakers online
8:00+   Castle upgrade (T3) if the game goes long
```

Human strategy vs Orc is built around the **fast expand** (FE). The Archmage's
Brilliance Aura lets the expanded base out-tech and out-eco the Orc by mid-game.
The key question is whether BM harassment delays the FE by more than ~60 s — if so,
the entire Archmage FE economic advantage evaporates.

Variant — **Mountain King opener:** MK first instead of AM; more aggressive early.
Delays FE but can trade better vs BM harassment. Less common at top level in OvH
because AM FE is the dominant strategy.

## Win conditions — Orc

- **BM harassment window:** Blademaster needs to kill 2+ peasants or force a late
  FE before 5:00. If the Human FE goes up uncontested at 4:30, the eco gap grows
  each minute. Every second Orc delays the Human expo is worth ~20 gold/min.
- **Power spike:** Stronghold + Bloodlust (Shaman) + 6–8 Raiders at 5:30–6:00.
  Ensnare cancels Footmen's Shield defense; Bloodlust Raiders shred Sorceresses.
- **Deny FE:** Kodo Drum war stomp + Raider ensnare kills FE buildings before
  they are reinforced. Time this at 5:30–6:30 before Spellbreakers arrive.
- **2nd hero pressure:** Far Seer wolves + BM at level 3 creates an XP and gold
  drain vs the expanding Human before the AM economy kicks in.
- **Burrow micro:** Orc Burrows (ranged mode) supplement defense and let peons
  repair; keep at least 3–4 active burrows.

## Win conditions — Human

- **AM FE economy:** The Archmage Brilliance Aura doubles mana regen; with an
  expanded Arcane Sanctum the Human out-techs Orc by minute 7–8.
- **Fast expand timing:** FE must go up before 5:00. Each 60 s delay costs ~300–400
  gold across the game. If BM kills the FE, the Human must go keep-pressure instead.
- **Sorceress mass:** Sorceress slow + Polymorph removes Raiders from fights;
  Spell Breaker dispels Bloodlust. This combo hard-counters the Orc T2 army.
- **Mountain King Storm Bolt:** Stuns BM during Wind Walk, preventing escape.
  MK + AM hero combo kills the BM investment in mid-game fights.
- **Defend with militia:** Early game militia + Defend (Footman ability) absorbs
  BM wind-walk hits. Human should never leave the base undefended at 1:30–3:00.

## Common Orc mistakes

- **BM over-commitment:** Chasing footmen with Blade while militia kills it; BM must
  Wind Walk out when low. A dead BM at level 1–2 is game-losing vs HU FE.
- **Delayed Stronghold past 3:00:** Loses the Shaman + Raider spike window entirely;
  Human gets Sorceresses before Orc army is relevant.
- **Not denying the FE:** Letting Human FE go up uncontested at 4:30 and then trying
  to fight the 2-base Human at 8:00 — Orc cannot win the long game.
- **Floating gold at T2:** Not spending on Shaman adept training + Bloodlust + 2nd hero
  right when Stronghold completes. This is the Orc power window; missing it costs the
  fight.
- **Supply block during spike:** Not building Burrows before Stronghold completes;
  stalls Raider production at the critical 5:30–6:00 timing.

## Common Human mistakes

- **FE too greedy:** Expanding at 3:30 without 3+ Footmen or militia to defend;
  BM Wind Walk kills the expo before walls are up.
- **Neglecting militia:** Forgetting to call militia when BM walks in at 1:30–2:30.
  Free BM kills are what give Orc the tempo to match the Human eco advantage.
- **Skipping Blacksmith:** Blacksmith is required for Keep; delaying it past 1:30
  pushes Keep to 4:00+ which kills the T2 timing vs Orc's Shaman spike.
- **No Spell Breakers vs Bloodlust:** Going pure Sorceress without Spell Breakers;
  loses the Bloodlust Raider fight instantly. Spell Breakers must be added by minute 7.
- **Hero deaths:** AM is fragile; if BM catches AM alone at level 1–2 and kills it,
  the FE economy loses its enabler (Brilliance Aura). Keep AM safe early.
