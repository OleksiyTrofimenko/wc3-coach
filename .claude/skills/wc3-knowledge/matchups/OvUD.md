# Matchup: Orc vs Undead (OvUD) — patch 2.0

**Provenance:** High-ladder community knowledge, Grubby/Lyn/Soin-era references,
W3Champions match analyses. `verified: community`. Patch: 2.0 (Reforged 2.00, build 6117).
Added by Strategist in T3.2.

---

## Standard Orc build order (OvUD) — Blademaster or Far Seer opener

```
0:00   5 peons start mining
0:15   peon → build Altar of Storms
0:30   peon → build Barracks
~0:45  resume 6th peon
1:00   Barracks complete → Blademaster (or Far Seer — see variants)
1:00   peon → build Orc Burrow (food)
1:30   Blademaster out → creep small camp (DO NOT harass UD base early)
2:00–2:15  Stronghold upgrade begins
2:30   2nd Orc Burrow
3:15   Stronghold complete → Shaman / Raider / Kodo production
3:30–4:00  2nd hero (Far Seer wolves or Shadow Hunter healing)
5:00–5:30  Expansion (safer vs UD than vs HU; DK creep route is predictable)
```

Orc vs Undead is a matchup where **map control and creep racing** determine the
mid-game winner. The Orc BM can contest UD creep camps to deny the Death Knight XP,
but the UD Death Knight's Unholy Aura makes any confrontation punishing.

Variant — **Far Seer first (FivO):** Far Seer wolves creep aggressively and contest
UD camps; less harassment pressure but faster XP accumulation. Uncommon but valid
on creep-heavy maps.

Variant — **Fast Expo Orc:** Expansion at ~4:00 if DK is confirmed creeping far away.
Risky if UD plays Undead fast attack with Ghouls + DK.

## Standard Undead build order (UDvO) — Death Knight opener

```
0:00   3 acolytes start haunting gold mines; 1 acolyte → build Altar of Darkness
0:15   Altar of Darkness begins
0:30   acolyte → build Crypt (required for Ghouls)
~0:45  4th acolyte starts mining; 5th queued from Necropolis
1:00   Altar complete → Death Knight
1:10   Crypt complete → Ghoul production begins (1–2 Ghouls)
1:30–2:00  Death Knight out → small creep or harass Orc workers
2:00   acolyte → build Graveyard (enables Halls of the Dead upgrade)
2:30   Necropolis → Halls of the Dead (T2) upgrade begins
         [Halls of the Dead: 100 gold, 180 lumber, 65 s build; reference ~2:30–3:00]
3:00   Halls of the Dead complete → Necromancer / Crypt Fiend / Lich available
3:30   2nd hero: Lich (standard) or Dread Lord
4:00   Spirit Tower for defense; Slaughterhouse if Meat Wagons needed
7:00+  Expansion (Haunted Gold Mine; fast ~20 s setup, but late due to DK focus)
```

Undead standard in OvUD is **DK + Lich** with Ghouls and Crypt Fiends. The Death
Knight's Death Coil and Unholy Aura allow safe solo-creeping and sustained
harassment. Crypt Fiends add a web ability (aerial denial) and ranged pierce
damage that punishes Orc Raiders.

Variant — **DK + Dread Lord:** Dread Lord Sleep + Vampiric Aura for sustained
army fights. Less common vs Orc but valid in longer games.

Variant — **Necromancer mass:** Necromancers + Skeletal Warriors as meat shield.
Skeleton upgrade scales poorly vs Orc dispel (Witch Doctor hex, Shaman Purge),
so this is a niche strategy.

## Win conditions — Orc

- **Deny DK XP:** BM contests mid-tier creep camps (4–8 gold bounty) where the
  DK wants to level. A DK stuck at level 2 at 6:00 is a massive disadvantage for UD.
- **Power spike:** Stronghold + Bloodlust + 6–8 Raiders + Kodo Drum at 5:30–6:30.
  Kodo War Stomp stuns Ghouls/Crypt Fiends; ensnare grounds flying units.
- **Shaman Purge vs Lich:** Purge removes Frost Armor (UD's primary damage
  mitigation ability) from units, making the UD army take full damage.
- **Far Seer wolves control space:** Wolves block UD Ghouls from efficiently
  chasing and deny map vision; useful on maps with narrow choke points.
- **Early expo pressure:** If Orc secures expansion at 5:30 while UD is still on
  one base, the Orc economic lead compounds — UD cannot match 2-base Orc at 8:00.

## Win conditions — Undead

- **DK Death Coil sustain:** DK sustains the UD army indefinitely in creep fights;
  Orc has no comparable single-unit sustain at the same tier.
- **Crypt Fiend web:** Web grounds Orc Raiders (flying) and Wind Riders; kills
  the Orc air harassment option entirely.
- **Lich Frost Nova:** AoE slow + damage that kites the Orc melee army. Combined
  with Unholy Aura speed advantage, UD can disengage from unfavorable fights.
- **Necromancer Skeletal Warriors (mid-game):** Mass skeletons with Skeleton
  Warrior upgrade act as a disposable front line that absorbs Orc physical damage
  while Lich/DK deal AoE from behind.
- **Death Pact on corpses:** If Orc is forced to fight in or near UD base, DK
  Death Pact on own Ghouls heals for massive amounts, prolonging fights.

## Common Orc mistakes

- **Harassing UD base at 1:30:** UD Crypt (Ghouls) + Spirit Tower defense makes
  early BM base harassment very costly; unlike vs NE wisps, UD workers (Acolytes)
  do not die to BM alone and Spirit Towers are up fast. Harass only if 100% safe.
- **Losing BM to DK Death Coil chain:** BM at low HP near UD army + DK at mana
  is a kill combo. BM must Wind Walk out when Death Coil comes off cooldown.
- **No Shaman Purge vs Frost Armor:** Skipping Shaman adept training means the
  UD army keeps Frost Armor on all units; Orc physical damage drops by ~30%.
- **Floating gold after Stronghold:** Same pattern as OvNE — not spending immediately
  on 2nd hero + Bloodlust + Kodo production loses the T2 window.
- **Letting DK free-level to 5:** A DK at level 5 with Unholy Aura 3 and Death
  Coil 3 is a near-unkillable sustain engine; Orc must contest camps or win before DK hits 5.

## Common Undead mistakes

- **Delayed Halls of the Dead past 3:30:** Loses access to Crypt Fiends and Lich
  while Orc Stronghold completes; UD army is pure Ghouls vs Bloodlust Raiders — a
  losing fight.
- **Skipping Spirit Towers:** BM Wind Walk kills exposed Acolytes; 2–3 Spirit
  Towers at the mineral line stop this cold. One Spirit Tower at the gold mine by 2:00
  is mandatory vs BM.
- **No Crypt Fiend web vs air:** UD without Crypt Fiends cannot stop Orc Wind Riders
  in the late game; web is the answer and must be produced before Wind Riders appear.
- **Over-stacking Necromancers vs Purge:** Shaman Purge removes Skeletal Warriors;
  mass Necromancer investment is poor vs Orc because the skeletons vanish on Purge.
- **DK dying in mid-game fights:** DK is UD's primary sustain and XP lead; losing it
  to a Kodo War Stomp + BM combo in an open-field fight is often game-ending.
