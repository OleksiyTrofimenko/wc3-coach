# Glossary

- **creep / creeping** — killing neutral units for XP/gold/items.
- **expand (FE)** — building a second town hall / fast expansion.
- **tech (T2/T3)** — upgrading the main building to unlock units/upgrades.
- **harass** — pressuring the opponent's economy with small forces.
- **tower rush (TR)** — early aggressive building near the enemy base.
- **micro / macro** — unit control in fights / economy and production management.
- **APM / EPM** — actions / effective actions per minute.
- **supply block** — production halted by hitting the food cap.
